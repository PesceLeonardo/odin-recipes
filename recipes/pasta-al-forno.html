Pasta Al Forno

Pasta al forno (lit. "pasta to the oven", i.e. "baked pasta") or timballo di pasta is a typical dish of Italian cuisine, made of (usually short) pasta covered with béchamel sauce, tomato sauce and cheese, and cooked in the oven.

Baked pasta can ideally be divided in two big categories:
the version with béchamel sauce was born in the Renaissance courts of the center and north, as a poorer variant of meat pies, from which probably derive very famous dishes such as lasagne al forno and Emilian cannelloni;
the so-called pasta 'nfurnata or pasta 'ncasciata is instead one of the most typical dishes of Sicily (particularly of the province of Messina, in the specific of Mistretta, and of the province of Catania) and has its origins in very ancient traditions, essentially ascribable to the sumptuous timbales that Arabs introduced in Sicily during their domination dating back to the ninth century, to which, however, is due the name timballo itself.

Ingredients (for four people)

  • 350g of mezzi rigatoni pasta
  • 200g of sausages
  • 500g of tomato purée
  • 200ml of béchamel sauce
  • 250g of mozzarella cheese
  • 40g of grated cheese
  • ½ an onion
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt as needed

Steps

  1. In a pan, sauté half a chopped onion in olive oil.
  2. Break the sausages directly into the pan and brown them.
  3. Now add the tomato purée, season with salt to taste, and let it cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Meanwhile, as soon as the pasta water starts boiling, add salt and drop in the mezzi rigatoni.
  5. When the sauce is ready, turn off the heat and add the béchamel sauce, stirring well.
  6. Drain the pasta halfway through cooking and transfer it to the pan with the sauce.
  7. Now add the diced mozzarella and grated cheese, but set aside some mozzarella and cheese for the final gratin.
  8. Transfer the pasta to a baking dish and evenly distribute the remaining mozzarella and grated cheese on top.
  9. Bake in a ventilated oven at 180°C (356°F) for about 25 minutes or in a static oven at 190°C (374°F) for the same time.
  10. Your delicious pasta al forno is ready to be served!
